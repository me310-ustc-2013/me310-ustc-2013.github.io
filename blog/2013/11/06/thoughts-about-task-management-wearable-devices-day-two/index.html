Thoughts About Task Management Wearable Devices Day Two

Nov 6th, 2013

I have to be a hard worker those days. I should think what I will do tomorrow, or I would be caught unprepared when I finished the task at hand, found the materials was not in hand and ended up with wasting time, especially when I need to take the boring classes in the school, though I skipped most of them this term …

At this time, I am wondering what I should do. So I write those line down.

There always times that I know what I should do — they flash through my mind and I do not have the convenient way to store it. I do not want to take out my smartphone and write them down when walking in the street or brushing my teech or whatever so.

If at this time, there is a wearable device that is with all the time. I could just say and record them. Kind of like recording the idea of recording the flash or writers — I have need for such moment too. Somethimes I read somethings really interesting and it let me have some really interesting or important ideas. At those time, I must remind myself to take out my smartphone and write some stubs. But it is not convenient. If you have that experience, you will find out — it takes some willingness to do such job. Again, if there is a wearable device that let you conveniently record such flashing ideas, it would be great!

Ok, I will figure out what I should next not!
